# Privacy

Tally Up is built on a simple principle: **your financial data should remain under your control.**

---

## What stays on your device

All calculations — including forecasts, projections, and reports — are performed locally on your device. Your financial data is never sent to Tally Up's servers.

---

## Sync between your devices

Your data syncs across your devices via your own iCloud account. Tally Up has no involvement in this transfer — it goes directly through Apple's infrastructure under your Apple ID.

Market data (security prices and exchange rates) is fetched independently on each device and is not synced via iCloud. See [Securities](Market Data/Securities.md) and [FX Rates](Market Data/FX Rates.md) for details.

---

## Privacy Policy

For full details, see the Privacy Policy linked in the Store screen within the app.
